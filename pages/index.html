Ostium news is a service primarily aimed at blind people or people find the internet hard to use, but can be utilised by anyone. The service works by you calling our service on +441647632031. The service is free to use, but you will be charged at your standard UK landline call rate.

Features to be included.

  • Spoken aloud news service (BBC, CNN, ABC, Algazira, SKY, ITV/2/3/4, Channel 4/5, Times, Gurdian, Independant, Daily Mail, New scientist, Wonderpidia.)
  • Spoken aloud weather service
  • Spoken aloud train times
  • Chose the news paper you want or just ask for general news
  • Disabeld car park directory
  • Blind taxi service
  • Map games
  • Fact of the day
  • App of the week
  • What to do service
  • Ringtone of the week
